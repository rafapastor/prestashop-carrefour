# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Summary

- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, not people.
- **Be patient.** Contributors have different backgrounds, experience levels and first languages.
- **No harassment.** No personal attacks, no discrimination, no trolling, no public or private harassment.
- **Keep it technical in issues and PRs.** Off-topic political, religious or personal debates belong elsewhere.

## Enforcement

Reports of unacceptable behaviour can be sent to **carrefour@smart-shop-ai.com**. Reports are confidential.

Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban, depending on severity. The maintainer's decision is final.
